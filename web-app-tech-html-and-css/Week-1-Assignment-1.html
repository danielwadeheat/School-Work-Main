A New Life Start is Right Here, Not Left There

There is no better time to start a new life than right now. The past has passed, and we must allow it to be left there, so we can be right here. The future is just a culmination of now consistently. The present is the only time that you can control. So, why not start a new life today? Do not wait until later today... well, unless you have prior obligations. You can work on new principles though even then throughout. You are the best at whatever you are doing right NOW.

Just do it

You can do it, if anyone else has done it or if you can dream it. You have the power to change your life. You have the power to change your future. You have the power to change your destiny. You have the power to change your world. You have the power to change. So, just... you know the rest. Do it.


In Consistency Lies the Power

We overestimate how much we can do at once, and underestimate how much we can do over time. You work out your mind just like your body, and, just like workouts, you do not want to overdo it, causing injury and setback. Done is better than perfect. So, do it with consistent mental hypertrophy, stopping one rep from failure. You learn more as you do more. If you do not do things consistently though, you only half-learn. By trying to be too perfect, sometimes things end up only half-fini...


Be Forgetful

If you are worried about something that might happen in the future, you delay getting something done in the present, giving more time for what you fear to possibly happen. Fear does not stop anything from happening. Quit worrying about what might never happen. Even if it is something you know is inevitable, sometimes all you can do is just tell yourself to forget about it, in the words of Fat Tony, and deal with it when it gets here. The fear of something can stop you from being in the moment, where the most growth is found. Sometimes we fear how much we think we have to remember, and that fear causes us to forget, but if we let go and just forget, our minds can be more clear to remember what we need when that moment comes. So, if there's anyting you need to remember from this section, forget it.

Turn it off

Sometimes we think too much. Instead of letting fear of failure keep you from trying, try telling your mind to obey the dreams you have in your heart, despite the fear of the unknown. Most often, fears and limits are just illusions, as Michael Jordan said before. When you choose to take a step toward your dreams, despite the fear, you find a new strength through a better view that's only found on that step. Each step then creates a clearer and bigger view.
